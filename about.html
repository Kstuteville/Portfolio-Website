About Me

About Veilbound

Veilbound is my signature digital project in the works, blending cinematic visuals, game design, machine learning, and interactive storytelling. Veilbound is a fantasy-horror action-adventure game featuring a female exorcist, Inara, navigating a cursed kingdom plagued by spirits, demons, and forbidden rituals. With a focus on emotionally reactive AI, moral decision-making, and atmospheric storytelling, Veilbound combines cinematic combat with immersive exploration in a vertical slice designed for thesis research and portfolio presentation.
