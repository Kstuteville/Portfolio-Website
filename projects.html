Featured Project

Veilbound

A Dark Fantasy Action-Adventure RPG

Veilbound is my flagship project—a cinematic 3D RPG that pushes the boundaries of interactive storytelling through adaptive AI and emotionally responsive gameplay. Set in a cursed kingdom plagued by supernatural forces, players embody Inara, a female exorcist navigating a world where every decision shapes not just the story, but how the world itself responds to your presence.
